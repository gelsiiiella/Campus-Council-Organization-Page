Tugon Party Alliance

Nagkakaisang Tugon Party Alliance is a distinguished political entity within the University of the East-Caloocan. Our paramount objective is to cultivate a cadre of adept and dedicated leaders who are fervently committed to serving the student body and advocating for their welfare. Through a holistic approach to leadership development and an unwavering dedication to the principles of integrity and excellence, we endeavor to empower our members to become catalysts for positive change within our university community and beyond. With a rich tradition of service and a steadfast commitment to our core values, Nagkakaisang Tugon Party Alliance stands as a beacon of hope and progress, driving forward the aspirations of our fellow students and championing their rights and interests.
