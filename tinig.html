Tinig Party Alliance

Tunay. Palaban. Makabayan. These words embody the essence of our organization, the longest-serving and ever-resilient community-based entity within the halls of UE Caloocan since its establishment in 1981. With a rich and storied history rooted in steadfast dedication to student welfare and advocacy, we stand as a beacon of unwavering commitment to our fellow students and the broader community. For over four decades, we have remained at the forefront of student activism and social engagement, tirelessly championing the rights and interests of our constituents. Our legacy is one of resilience, courage, and unwavering dedication to the principles of social justice and equality. As we continue our journey forward, we remain steadfast in our mission to uphold the values of truth, empowerment, and solidarity, ensuring that the voice of the student body resonates loud and clear, now and for generations to come.

General Plan of Actions


The Sulong Komite, also known as the Engineering Student Committee, is the governing body of the EN Student Council and works as its progressive leaders.

A holistic approach towards addressing concerns and issues, emphasizing innovation, inclusivity and excellence in education, with a focus on students' rights and welfare.

Surveys and evaluations are carried out as part of Student Assessment to have a deeper understanding about needsd and concerns of the students. Based on the gathered data, plans can be put in place to offer proactive support for students while helping them overcome different difficulties.

Routine inspections and monitoring of the Engineering Department's facilities and laboratories.The council will quickly identify any possible problem in this way to ensure that corrective measures are taken.

The State of the Student Address serves as a formal congress for communication and dialogue between the student body and college officials. These discussions encompass academic policies, campus facilities, student services, and more.

ENspacio aims to create additional learning environments to maximize educational resources through careful assessment and redesign, these underutilize rooms will be transformed into multifunctional hubs for collaboration, creativity, and engagement.
